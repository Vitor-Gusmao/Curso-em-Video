Background configs

Size:

  • "Background-size: cover;" Cover the screen 100% with the image
  • "Background-size: auto;" fits the image automatically on your screen
  • "Background-size: contain;" show 100% of the image on your screen, but dont fit it all/li>
  • "Background-size: 100% 100%;" show 100% of the image on your screen and fit it.

Attachment:

  • "Background-attachment: scroll;" scroll the image together with the content.
  • "Background-attachment: fixed;" fits perfectly with the website.

Shorthand:

background: color > image > position > repeat > attachment

SIZE DO NOT WORK ON SHORTHAND

Example:

"Background: black url(image.png) center center no-repeat fixed;
Background-size: cover;"
